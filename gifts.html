Gift Certificates

Our beautiful gift certificates can be used on anything within the greenhouse year-round except for at the annual art show. They can be any amount and they don’t expire. If you don’t live in the area, just give us a call—we can do everything over the phone and have it delivered with regular, next-day post or express mail to ensure it arrives in time!

When calling to order a gift certificate, we will need the following Information:

  • Your phone number
  • Your mailing address
  • Amount for the gift certificate
  • Visa or Mastercard number
  • CV Code on the back of your card
  • Personal message you would like to include
  • Name and address of recipient
